Why I Started One Hundred Percent Human

Now more than ever we need content and media generated by humans, Artificial Intelligence can now replicate a human author's writing style, if you feed an AI model any scene from a film it can nearly now nearly perfectly regenerate that scene. At this very point in time is precisely where human made content is being outnumbered and overpowered by the sheer amount of AI generated content on the internet. Human generated content is unsurprisingly is being paid for by the billions to train AI models, and willingly and unsurprisingly being handed over. It won't be long until corporations discover how much cheaper is to generate things like movies, tv shows and adverstisements with AI, this isn't just happening in the film industry either, any other creative medium on this Earth is under threat, nearly every medium will soon be overrun with AI generated content, this is precisely why every artist needs to create or rather be inspired to create something us, only humans can understand, this is why i created this One hundred percent human films, to inspire and help other humans find their inner power and desire to create.
